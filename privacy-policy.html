​Privacy Policy

​Okay, here's a more human-sounding version of that privacy policy:

1.  What Info We Grab

We only grab the info we need to make things better for you. This might be:

*   Stuff you give us straight up, like your name, email, or what you say in our forms.
*   Techy stuff, like your IP address, what kind of browser you're using, what device you're on, and what pages you peep.
*   Cookies – little files that help us remember what you like and how you use the site.

We don't grab any extra private stuff we don't need.

2.  How We Use Your Info

We use your info to:

*   Keep the website running smoothly
*   Chat with you when you reach out
*   Make the site and content better
*   Send you updates you asked for
*   Follow the rules and laws

We'll never sell or rent your personal info, plain and simple.

3.  How We Keep Your Data Safe

We use security stuff like encryption and secure servers to keep your info safe.

Keep in mind that no way of sending data online is 100% bulletproof.

4.  Cookies

Cookies are small files that help our website remember your preferences and improve usability.
You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings if you prefer not to allow them.

5.  Sharing Info

We might share some info in these cases:

*   With trusted folks who help us run the website
*   If the law says we have to
*   To protect our rights, safety, or users

These other companies have to keep your data safe and only use it for the reasons we let them.

6.  Your Rights

You're in charge of your data. You can:

*   Ask for a copy of the info we have on you
*   Tell us to fix or delete your info
*   Say you don't want us to contact you

Hit us up if you have any questions or complaints about your privacy.

7.  Links to Other Sites

Our site might have links to other websites.

We’re not responsible for what those sites do with your privacy or what's on those sites. Check their privacy policies before you share anything.

8.  Changes to This Policy

We might update this Privacy Policy every so often.

If we do, we’ll post the new version on this page with a new date. Check back now and then to stay in the loop.
